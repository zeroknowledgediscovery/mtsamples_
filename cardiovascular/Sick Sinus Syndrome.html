Description: Sick sinus syndrome, atrial fibrillation, pacemaker dependent, mild cardiomyopathy with ejection fraction 40% and no significant decompensation, and dementia of Alzheimer's disease with short and long term memory dysfunction (Medical Transcription Sample Report)

He also has a fairly advanced degree of Alzheimer's dementia and is living in an assisted care facility.
